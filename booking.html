Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the cruise price?

Your cruise fare includes accommodation, all meals in main dining venues, select beverages, entertainment, and access to most onboard facilities. Additional charges apply for specialty dining, premium beverages, shore excursions, and spa treatments.

How do I pay for my cruise?

After submitting your booking request, our team will contact you within 24 hours to confirm availability and process payment. We accept all major credit cards and offer installment payment plans.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 30+ days before departure receive a full refund. Cancellations 15-29 days before departure receive a 50% refund. Cancellations less than 15 days before departure are non-refundable.

Do I need a passport?

Yes, a valid passport is required for all international cruises. Your passport must be valid for at least 6 months beyond your travel dates.
